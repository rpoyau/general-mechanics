\subsection{Thermodynamic representation}
Choose resource coordinates
\[
  R \;=\; (E,\, V,\, N_1,\ldots,N_k),
  \qquad
  \rho \;=\; S .
\]
Define the conjugates by
\[
  \Lambda_E \;=\; -\frac{1}{T},
  \qquad
  \Lambda_V \;=\; -\frac{p}{T},
  \qquad
  \Lambda_{N_i} \;=\; +\frac{\mu_i}{T}.
\]
Substituting these into the resourceâ€“space identity \eqref{eq:reos} gives the Gibbs differential:
\begin{equation}
  dS
  \;=\;
  \frac{1}{T}\, dE
  \;+\;
  \frac{p}{T}\, dV
  \;-\;
  \sum_{i=1}^{k} \frac{\mu_i}{T}\, dN_i .
  \label{eq:gibbs-entropy-form}
\end{equation}

\subsection{Other choices}
Any declared list $R^{(a)}$ determines its own naming via
\[
  \Lambda_a(R) \;=\; -\,\partial_{R^{(a)}} \rho(R).
\]
