\section{Unit-Agnostic View}
\label{sec:unit_agnostic}

\noindent
The boxed corollaries in Secs.~\ref{sec:continuity}–\ref{sec:synthesis}
were derived in Kick units (\(c=\hbar=k_{B}=4\pi\varepsilon_{0}=1\)).
To translate any equation back to \emph{explicit‐constant form}:

\begin{itemize}
\item Multiply each entropy density by \(k_{B}\ln 2\)\;[J K\(^{-1}\)\,m\(^{-n}\)].  
\item Replace every energy variable \(E\) by \(h\nu\)\;[J].  
\item Insert factors of \(c\) to restore dimensions in momentum and
      length (\(p \!\to p\,c,\;x\!\to x\,/c\)).  
\item Convert the dimensionless charge \(e=\sqrt{\alpha}\) to coulombs
      via \(e\!\to\!e\sqrt{4\pi\varepsilon_{0}\hbar c}\).
\end{itemize}

No boxed identity changes shape: scale factors cancel inside every
gradient, divergence, and Legendre transform.  Thus the relational
structure of the REOS axiom is \emph{unit-agnostic}; Kick gauge merely
hides the constants for algebraic convenience.
