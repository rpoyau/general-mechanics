\subsection{Aim and stance}
\label{introduction:aim-stance}
The Relational Equation of State (REOS) treats observed mechanics as relational statements extracted from a single geometric identity on an informational manifold: the generalized Stokes/continuity statement \eqref{axioms:stokes:identity:eq}–\eqref{axioms:stokes:identity:eq:local-continuity}. An observer specifies a region \(U\subset M\) with boundary \(\partial U\); quantities are read from boundaries.
