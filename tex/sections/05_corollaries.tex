\section{Corollaries}
\label{corollaries}

\noindent
This section collects short, compute–first consequences of the Stokes/continuity identity
\(d(*j)=\sigma\,\mathrm{vol}\) together with the \emph{Frame} closure (when invoked), organized as a ladder.
The core rungs (\S\ref{corollary:info-gas}–\S\ref{corollary:geometry}) are where most readers should start; \S\ref{corollary:dimensional}–\S\ref{corollary:qpi-template} are optional extensions that
build on the same spine.

\begin{itemize}\itemsep2pt
  \item \textbf{Core (\S\ref{corollary:info-gas}–\S\ref{corollary:geometry}):} info–gas on \emph{Boundaries}; \emph{Frame} inverse–area law; continuity \& Clausius;
  external drives with delay/production; Info–EM waves \& export; time–information bound;
  noise floor; geometry equivalence.
\item \textbf{Extensions (\S\ref{corollary:dimensional}–\S\ref{corollary:qpi-template}):} dimensional flows \& confinement; thermo/Newton rate laws from
  the identity; least–action as a boundary‑rate equivalence; calibrated entropic corrections; a QPI
  template (optional modelling choice).
\end{itemize}
