\begin{abstract}
We introduce \emph{General Mechanics}, grounded in three pillars: Machian relationism, information-theoretic modelling (Shannon), and geometric calculus. Their synthesis yields informational kinematics and a \emph{Thermodynamics Relational Equation of State (REOS)}. A Stokes-type balance on informational currents gives continuity identities; the differential REOS $\mathrm d\rho=-\Lambda_a\,\mathrm dR^{(a)}$ organizes dynamics as \emph{prices} $\Lambda_a$ conjugate to \emph{relations} $R^{(a)}$. Familiar structures (Lagrangian/Hamiltonian form, Maxwell-style identities) appear as algebraic corollaries.
\end{abstract}

\smallskip
\noindent\textbf{Keywords:} General Mechanics, relational information, Shannon information, generalized Stokes balance, REOS, informational kinematics, variational principles, Maxwell-form.
