\subsection{Info–EM (Maxwell form)}
\label{corollary:info-em:maxwell}

\paragraph{Setup.}
On a wave window with the wave–supporting closure (\eqref{axioms:waves:eq:chi}–\eqref{axioms:waves:eq:balance}),
choose an informational potential $A_I$ (a $1$–form) and define the field $2$–form $F_I := dA_I$.
Let the excitation be $H_I := \chi : F_I$ (medium/model choice). Boundary orientation is as in §2.1.

\paragraph{Derivation.}
From $F_I=dA_I$ (identity of exterior calculus), $dF_I=0$.
With $H_I=\chi:F_I$, define the current $3$–form $J_I := dH_I$; then $dH_I = J_I$ and $dJ_I=0$,
consistent with the continuity/Stokes family (\eqref{axioms:stokes:identity:eq}–\eqref{axioms:stokes:identity:eq:local-continuity}).
The Maxwell system on the window is
\begin{equation}
\boxed{\, dF_I = 0, \qquad dH_I = J_I \,}
\label{corollary:info-em:maxwell:eq:differential}
\end{equation}
with $H_I=\chi:F_I$ supplying the constitutive relation.

\paragraph{Specialization.}
Under a $(3{+}1)$ split identifying $F_I \leftrightarrow (\mathbf E,\mathbf B)$ and
$H_I \leftrightarrow (\mathbf D,\mathbf H)$, \eqref{corollary:info-em:maxwell:eq:differential} reduces to
\begin{equation}
\nabla\!\cdot\mathbf B=0,\quad
\nabla\times\mathbf E+\partial_t\mathbf B=0,\quad
\nabla\!\cdot\mathbf D=\rho,\quad
\nabla\times\mathbf H-\partial_t\mathbf D=\mathbf J ,
\label{corollary:info-em:maxwell:eq:components}
\end{equation}
where $J_I \leftrightarrow (\rho,\mathbf J)$. Poynting’s theorem is the instance of
(\eqref{axioms:waves:eq:energy}–\eqref{axioms:waves:eq:balance}) with
$u=\tfrac12(\mathbf E\!\cdot\!\mathbf D+\mathbf B\!\cdot\!\mathbf H)$ and $\mathbf S=\mathbf E\times\mathbf H$.
